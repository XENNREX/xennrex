Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you commit.

How does the 20 GB entry plan work? ▼

Our entry tier gives you 20 GB of secure storage for a one-time or monthly fee. It's perfect for testing the platform or storing essential documents. Upgrade anytime as your needs grow.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? ▼

Absolutely. You can change your plan at any time from your account dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my transfer quota? ▼

We'll notify you when you hit 80% of your quota. You can purchase additional transfer in 100 GB blocks, or upgrade to a higher plan for better value.

Is there a money-back guarantee? ▼

Yes. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If Xennrex Cloud isn't right for you, contact support for a full refund — no questions asked.

How secure is my data really? ▼

Every file is encrypted with AES-256 on your device using keys only you possess. We use RAID1 mirrored storage, daily encrypted backups to Backblaze B2, and our servers are protected by UFW firewall, Fail2ban, and geoIP blocking. Even we can't read your files.
