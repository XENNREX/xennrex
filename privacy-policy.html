3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Service provision: To create and manage your account, provide cloud storage, and deliver requested features
  • Communication: To send service notifications, security alerts, and support responses
  • Billing: To process payments, send invoices, and manage subscriptions
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and abuse
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our Services (using anonymized data only)
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored on dedicated servers located in South Africa. Backup copies may be stored in secure, encrypted facilities. We ensure that all data transfers comply with POPIA requirements.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for data in transit
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) enforcement
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • Automated threat detection and intrusion prevention
  • Geographic access restrictions

4.3 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our Services and comply with legal obligations. Upon account termination, we retain your data for 30 days to allow for export, after which it is permanently deleted.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: With trusted third parties who assist in operating our Services (e.g., payment processors, hosting providers), under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or legal process
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to you
  • With your consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share information

6. Your Rights Under POPIA

As a data subject under POPIA, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of your personal information we hold
  • Right to correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to object: Object to processing of your information for direct marketing
  • Right to restrict processing: Request limitation on how we use your information
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint: File a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@xennrex.github.io. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Prevent fraud and enhance security

8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

While our primary data storage is in South Africa, some service providers may process data outside South Africa. In such cases, we ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Encryption during transfer

10. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will:

  • Notify the Information Regulator within 72 hours
  • Notify affected Users without undue delay
  • Provide details of the breach and remediation steps
  • Take all reasonable steps to mitigate harm
