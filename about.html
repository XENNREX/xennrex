Our Story

Xennrex Cloud was born from a simple observation: South African businesses and individuals deserve cloud services that respect their privacy, their currency, and their right to data sovereignty.

While global cloud providers offer powerful platforms, they come with significant trade-offs for South Africans: data stored overseas, latency issues, USD billing with fluctuating exchange rates, and most critically — your data subject to foreign jurisdictions and surveillance laws.

We built Xennrex to solve these problems. By deploying Raspberry Pi 4 hardware locally and running open-source Nextcloud software, we offer a truly private cloud experience where:

  • Your data never leaves South African soil
  • Sub-50ms latency from anywhere in the country
  • Billing in ZAR — no currency surprises
  • Zero third-party access to your files
  • Open-source stack with no vendor lock-in

Our Mission

To empower South Africans with private, sovereign cloud infrastructure that keeps data local, secure, and under the owner's complete control — while supporting the local economy through ZAR billing and South African jobs.
