POPI Act Compliance

The Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) is South Africa's data privacy law. As a responsible party under POPIA, Xennrex ensures all personal information is processed lawfully, minimised to what is necessary, stored securely, and never transferred outside South Africa without authorisation. We handle all eight POPIA conditions for lawful processing: Accountability, Processing Limitation, Purpose Specification, Further Processing Limitation, Information Quality, Openness, Security Safeguards, and Data Subject Participation.

Your Data Never Leaves SA

Unlike international providers, your data is stored exclusively on infrastructure physically located within South Africa. This means:
