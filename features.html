Your private Nextcloud instance comes packed with enterprise-grade features — all running on your dedicated hardware in South Africa.

File Management

The core of your private cloud. Store, sync, and share files with complete control. Your data never leaves your dedicated hardware.

  • Automatic sync across all devices
  • Share via public or password-protected links
  • File versioning — recover older versions
  • Trash bin with configurable retention
  • WebDAV support for any client
  • End-to-end encryption option

Collaboration

Edit documents, spreadsheets, and presentations directly in your browser — no Microsoft Office license required.

  • Collabora Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • OnlyOffice integration
  • Built-in PDF viewer
  • Plain text & markdown editor
  • Real-time collaborative editing
  • Comments & annotations

Communication

Nextcloud Talk gives you secure video conferencing, screen sharing, and messaging — all self-hosted on your Pi.

  • HD video & voice calls
  • Screen sharing
  • Group chats & channels
  • Voice messages
  • File sharing in conversations
  • No call limits or data harvesting

Calendar & Contacts

Full CalDAV and CardDAV support. Sync with your phone, Thunderbird, Apple Calendar, and any DAV client.

  • Multiple calendars with color coding
  • Shared calendars for teams
  • Meeting scheduling & invitations
  • Contact management with groups
  • Sync with iOS, Android, macOS
  • Birthday calendar auto-generation

Security

Enterprise-grade security hardened by default. Your Pi is locked down, hidden behind VPN, and monitored 24/7.

  • Two-factor authentication enforced
  • Brute-force attack protection
  • Geo-blocking by country
  • Hidden server info & headers
  • Tailscale VPN mesh network
  • Automated security updates

Project Management

Organize work with tasks, Kanban boards, and notes. Keep projects moving without leaving your cloud.

  • Task lists with due dates & priorities
  • Kanban boards (Deck app)
  • Rich text notes
  • Task assignment & sharing
  • Integration with Calendar
  • File attachments on tasks

Email

Nextcloud Mail connects your existing email accounts — Gmail, Outlook, or any IMAP server — into a unified, ad-free interface.

  • Multi-account email client
  • Unified inbox
  • PGP encryption support
  • Threaded conversations
  • Smart folders & search
  • No ads or data scanning
