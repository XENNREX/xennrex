Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Xennrex Cloud ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, company name, phone number, and delivery address.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by PayFast. We do not store card details.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information for security purposes.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and manage your cloud services
  • To process payments and send invoices
  • To communicate about your service status
  • To improve our infrastructure and security

4. Data Sovereignty

Your data is stored on your own Raspberry Pi 4 hardware, physically located at your premises. We do not have access to your files, documents, or personal data stored on your Nextcloud instance.

5. Third-Party Services

We use PayFast for payment processing, Tailscale for VPN connectivity, MEGA for encrypted backups, and Cloudflare for edge networking. Each provider has their own privacy policy.

6. Your Rights

Under POPIA, you have the right to access, correct, delete, and port your personal data. Contact us at privacy@xennrex.org.

7. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact our Information Officer at privacy@xennrex.org.
